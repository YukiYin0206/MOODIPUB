  • Grasshopper

    Ingradients

    30ml Mint liqueur
    30ml Cacao liqueur
    30ml Heavy cream

    Steps

    1. Shake all ingredients with ice in a Boston

    3. Garnish with fresh mint.

  • Alien Brain

    Ingradients

    30ml Peach liqueur
    15ml Baileys
    Several drops Blue curacao
    Several drops Grenadine syrup

    Steps

    1. Pour peach liqueur into shot glass.
    2. Layering Baileys on top.
    3. Drizzle in grenadine syrup.
    4. Adding a splash of blue curacao.
