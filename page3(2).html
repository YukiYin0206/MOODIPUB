SADNESS

  • Blue Storm

    Ingradients

    30ml Vodka
    45ml Blue curacao
    Sparkling water

    Steps

    1. Add vodka and blue curacao to a mixing glass filled with ice.
    2. Stir for 15 seconds.
    3. Strain into an ice-filled highball.
    4. Top up with sparkling water.
    5. Garnish with a lemon wheel.

  • Blue Moon

    Ingradients

    45ml Gin
    20ml Giffard liqueur
    20ml Lemon juice

    Steps

    1. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass filled with ice.
    2. Stir for 15 seconds.
    3. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
    4. Garnish with a lemon wheel.

  • Coconut Tree

    Ingradients

    30ml Rum
    20ml Blue curacao
    50ml Coconut milk
    15ml Lemon juice

    Steps

    1. Shake rum, blue curacao and lemon juice with ice in a Boston Shaker for 30 seconds.
    2. Strain into an ice-filled highball.
    3. Top up with coconut milk.
    4. Garnish with coconut flakes.
