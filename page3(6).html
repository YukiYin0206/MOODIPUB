GUILT/SHAME

  • Chinese Bamboo

    Ingradients

    30ml Chinese white wine
    30ml Bamboo liqueur
    15ml Lime juice

    Steps

    1. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass filled
    with ice.
    2. Stir for 15 seconds.
    3. Strain into an old fashion glass filled with ice.
    4. Garnish with several piece of bamboo leaves.

  • Cucumber Jimlet

    Ingradients

    30ml Blue curacao
    30ml Orange juice
    15ml Lime juice
    Sparkling water

    Steps

    1. Add blue curacao, orange juice and lime juice to a mixing glass filled with ice.
    2. Stir for 15 seconds.
    3. Strain into an ice-filled highball.
    4. Top up with sparkling water.
    5. Garnish with fresh mint and lime wheel.

  • Green Temptation

    Ingradients

    45ml Gin
    20ml Cucumber juice
    10ml Lime juice

    Steps

    1. Shake all ingredients with ice in a Boston
    Shaker for 30 seconds.
    2. Strain it into a chilled coupe glass.
    3. Garnish with a cucumber twist.
