HOPELESSNESS

  • Espresso Martini

    Ingradients

    50ml Vodka
    30ml Coffee liqueur
    30ml Espresso, freshly brewed
    10ml Simple syrup

    Steps

    1. Shake all ingredients with ice in a Boston
    Shaker for 30 seconds.
    2. Strain it into a chilled martini glass.
    3. Garnish with several coffee beans.

  • Chocolatini

    Ingradients

    20ml Vodka
    60ml Baileys
    10ml Hershey's syrup

    Steps

    1. Drizzle the Hershey's syrup insides of
    a chilled martini glass.
    2. Shake vodka and baileys with ice in a Boston
    Shaker for 30 seconds.
    3. Strain into the martini glass.
    4. Sprinkle some coco powder on the surface.
    5. Garnish with several marshmallows.

  • Whiskey Sour

    Ingradients

    60ml Bourbon whiskey
    30ml Lemon juice
    20ml Simple Syrup
    1 Egg white

    Steps

    1. Dry shake all ingredients (without ice) in a Boston Shaker to emulsify egg yolk.
    2. Add ice and shake again until well-chilled.
    3. Strain into an ice-filled old fashion glass.
    4. Add 2 dashes of aromatic bitters on top.
