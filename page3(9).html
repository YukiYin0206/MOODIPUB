ENVY/JEALOUSY

  • Apple Pie

    Ingradients

    45ml Gin
    15ml Lemon juice
    15ml Apple syrup
    Apple cider or soda

    Steps

    1. Shake all ingredients with ice in a Boston
    Shaker for 30 seconds.
    2. Strain into an ice-filled highball.
    3. Top up with apple cider or syrup.
    4. Garnish with several slices of apple.

  • Maple Moment

    Ingradients

    45ml Scotch whisky
    10ml Montenegro
    60ml Cranberry juice
    5ml Maple syrup
    1 Egg white

    Steps

    1. Dry shake all ingredients (without ice) in a Boston Shaker to emulsify egg white.
    2. Add ice and shake again until well-chilled.
    3. Strain it into a chilled coupe glass.
    4. Garnish with a maple leaf.

  • Painkiller

    Ingradients

    60ml Rum
    30ml Coconut milk
    60ml Pineapple juice
    30ml Orange juice

    Steps

    1. Shake all ingredients with ice in a Boston
    Shaker for 30 seconds.
    2. Strain into an ice-filled old fashion glass.
    3. Garnish with several slices of pineapple.
