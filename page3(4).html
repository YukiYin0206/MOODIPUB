  • Peach Bellini

    Ingradients

    Shampagne or dry sparkling wine
    100ml Water
    15ml Lemon juice
    15ml Simple syrup
    Peeled and diced peaches

    Steps

    1. Puree peaches, water, lemon juice and simple syrup in a blender until they turn into smooth.
    2. Fill a chilled champagne flute a quarter
    full of the puree.
    3. Top up with champagne or sparkling wine.
    4. Garnish with several slices of peach.

  • Red Eye

    Ingradients

    Beer
    100ml Water
    15ml Lemon juice
    Peeled Cherry tomato

    Steps

    1. Puree cherry tomato, water and lemon juice
    in a blender until they turn into smooth.
    2. Fill an ice-filled highball a quarter
    full of the puree.
    3. Top up with beer.
    4. Garnish with halved cherry tomato.

  • Screwdriver

    Ingradients

    30ml Vodka
    90ml Orange juice

    Steps

    1. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass filled
    with ice.
    2. Stir for 15 seconds.
    3. Strain into an ice-filled old fashion glass.
    4. Garnish with an orange peel.
