  • White Angle

    Ingradients

    30ml Cocunut rum
    60ml Baileys
    90ml Milk

    Steps

    1. Shake all ingredients with ice in a Boston
    Shaker for 30 seconds.
    2. Strain it into a chilled coupe glass.
    3. Sprinkle icing on the surface.

  • Ice Breaker

    Ingradients

    45ml Tequila
    20ml Cointreau
    30ml Grapefruit juice
    10ml Grenadine syrup

    Steps

    1. Shake all ingredients with ice in a Boston
    Shaker for 30 seconds.
    2. Strain into an ice-filled old fashion glass.
    3. Garnish with a slice of grapefruit.

  • Yuzu

    Ingradients

    60ml Gin
    30ml Yuzu juice
    10ml Simple syrup
    Sparkling water

    Steps

    1. Shake gin, yuzu juice and simple syrup with
    ice in a Boston Shaker for 30 seconds.
    2. Strain into an ice-filled highball.
    3. Top up with sparkling water.
    4. Garnish with yuzu pulp.
