LOVE/ATTACHMENT

  • Wild Rose

    Ingradients

    50ml Jägermeister
    50ml Rose tea
    30ml Blackcurrant juice
    10ml Grenadine syrup

    Steps

    1. Shake all ingredients with ice in a Boston
    Shaker for 30 seconds.
    2. Strain into an old fashion glass filled with ice.
    3. Garnish with several dried rose petals.

  • Cosmopolitan

    Ingradients

    45ml Vodka
    15ml Cointreau
    30ml Cranberry juice
    15ml Lime juice

    Steps

    1. Shake all ingredients with ice in a Boston
    Shaker for 30 seconds.
    2. Strain into a chilled coupe glass.
    3. Garnish with a flamed orange peel.

  • Strawberry Daiquiri

    Ingradients

    45ml Rum
    15ml Coconut rum
    30ml Lime juice
    20ml Starberry syrup

    Steps

    1. Shake all ingredients with ice in a Boston
    Shaker for 30 seconds.
    2. Strain into an ice-filled old fashion glass.
    3. Garnish with several slices of strawberry.
